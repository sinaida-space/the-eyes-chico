an interactive installation · sinaida · prague · 2026

    
You are standing above a field that watches back. Descend. Fifty of the
    flowers glow — each one holds a question you can only answer in your own
    head. Nothing is recorded. The field only asks.


    
// HOW TO MOVE

    

      
HAND TRACKING (your camera, processed locally — nothing leaves this device)

      
✋ OPEN PALM — glide forward

      ✊ FIST, hold — turn (always clockwise, release to stop)

      ✌️ PEACE, hold — dive into the field / rise back out

      👍 THUMBS UP near a glowing flower — pick its question

      🤟 I LOVE YOU — forget the question; it sinks and never resurfaces

    

    

      
TOUCH: drag = steer · pinch = dive/rise · tap glowing flower = pick · tap = forget the open question

      KEYS/MOUSE: WASD or arrows = steer · mouse wheel = dive/rise · E / click = pick · any key forgets the open question · ESC = exit menu

The experience adapts to your hardware. HIGH: full nebula, dense field,
    VHS post-processing, hand tracking. MEDIUM: same world, leaner shaders. LOW/MOBILE:
    the essential journey — fewer eyes, calmer sky, touch controls — still the whole ritual.
    Headphones recommended on every tier.
