// WHAT THIS SITE DOES NOT DO

No analytics. No tracking pixels. No advertising. No third-party cookies. No accounts. Nothing you do here is transmitted to me or to anyone else.

// WHAT IT STORES (localStorage, on your device only)

— your consent choice
— which questions you have visited (your n/50 progress)
— your chosen quality tier
If you decline consent, nothing is stored and the experience simply forgets you between visits. You can erase everything at any time by clearing this site's data in your browser.

// CAMERA (hand tracking, optional)

If — and only if — you choose "enter with hand tracking", your browser asks for camera permission. The video is processed entirely inside your browser by MediaPipe (an on-device machine-learning library). No frame, landmark, or any derivative of the video ever leaves your device. Closing the tab or denying permission ends it.

// THIRD-PARTY REQUESTS

The site itself is self-contained (fonts and 3D engine are served with the site). Exception: choosing hand tracking downloads the MediaPipe model files from jsDelivr and Google's model storage — those CDNs see your IP address, as any download does. If you don't enable hand tracking, no third-party request is made. External links (Spotify, Instagram, sinaida.eu) are governed by their own policies.

// YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

Since no personal data reaches me, there is nothing for me to access, rectify, or delete on your behalf — the data lives only in your browser, under your control. For any question: sinaida.eu.
